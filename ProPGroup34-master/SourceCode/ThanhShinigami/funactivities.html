Games

Medieval tournaments

Armed and armored knights fight each other for prizes, and for the entertainment of the king and queen and the public! Rewards to the top three winners!

Making a Maypole

Once the maypole is set up, everyone can dance to a waltz, a polka, a jig, or any tune you choose. You can keep track of the braiding by chanting, "Go over it, then under it, and over it and under it," to keep from getting tangled in the ribbons.

King's Parade

Participate and march to the tune of the King's Parade! Anyone can join, come and show of your wonderful attire and demonstrate your loyalty to the realm!

Sword Fight Arena

Join the fun and pick up your weapon, anyone can participate! Fight your way to save the princess and get rewarded with a kiss upon your temple and a rose of gratitude. Be the knight of the day!
