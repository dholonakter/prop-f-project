Brief overview

LARPfestival is the first LARP event ever held by Events International. Events International is known for their big events at the Molecaten Park Kuierpad in Eindhoven City. There are special rules for this event. You have to dress in cosplays that fit medieval times! There's a whole lore behind the event, tons of activities, and many planned events. Click on the buttons for more information.
