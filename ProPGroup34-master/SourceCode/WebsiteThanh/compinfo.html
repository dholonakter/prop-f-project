About Events International

Event International is a modern company specialized in organizing and managing multi-day festivals and events.

Founded in 2014 in Eindhoven, the Netherlands, Event International has grown to one of the key companies in this area in the Netherlands and abroad.

Event International takes care of everything regarding your event, ranging from a website foradvertising the event, selling tickets, to automated support during the event or festival, and of courseour solution is always tailored to the client's needs.

According to Mr. Postma, the marketing manager of this company, Event International provides a complete solution for many events: We turn all your ideas into practice! You name the event, we take care of the organization!
